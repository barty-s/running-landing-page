I started running when I was around 20 to try and be a bit healthier and to enjoy the outdoors. I was living in Pisa at the time and would run along the riverside which was nice and flat. I kept running every now and then throughout my 20s but never took it very seriously or did any crazy distances.

But then, in my early 30s, I discovered I was secret fit!

From the other sports I'd been doing in my freetime, I realised I could run 7km easily... so I decided to challenge myself and signed up for the famous half marathon race from Vigo to Baiona, the Vig-Bay, in April 2022.

I'm here to tell you that if someone like me can finish a half marathon, then you can too! Sign up to my training programme today and I'll take you from absolute beginner to half marathon finisher.
